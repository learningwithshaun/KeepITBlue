About KeepITBlue

Our story, our people, and our drive to protect the ocean.

Our History

Founded in 2024 by a passionate group of environmental advocates, KeepITBlue began as a grassroots initiative focused on addressing the growing threat of ocean pollution. Recognising the critical need for action, the founding members — including Tiyani Ngwana, Amanda Msutu, Samukelo Ndela, Sikelela Ntingi, Zusiphe Mvovo, Abongile Phandle, environmentalists, and community leaders — came together to create an organisation dedicated to preserving marine ecosystems and raising public awareness.

Our first major project was a local beach cleanup in Cape Town, which saw overwhelming support from volunteers and local businesses. The success of this initiative inspired us to expand our reach — organising educational workshops, advocacy campaigns, and community-driven environmental projects.

Our Mission

To protect our oceans and beaches through community engagement, education, and innovative solutions that foster a sustainable future.

Our Vision

A world where our oceans are clean, healthy, and thriving — benefiting all forms of life and ensuring a sustainable future for generations to come.

Growth & Milestones

  • 2025: Launched the Blue Program, empowering local communities to take ownership of their coastlines through monthly cleanups and monitoring efforts.
  • 2026: Expanded operations internationally, establishing partnerships with environmental organisations in Australia, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
  • 2027: Introduced an innovative marine debris tracking system in collaboration with leading marine scientists to target high-risk areas for intervention.
  • 2028: Opened our first Ocean Education Centre in Cape Town, where students, tourists, and the public can learn about ocean ecosystems and conservation strategies.
