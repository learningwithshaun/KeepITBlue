We are KeepITBlue

KeepITBlue is a nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting our oceans and beaches. We work with local communities to reduce pollution, raise awareness, and promote sustainable practices that benefit both people and the environment.

About Us Contact

Our Mission

To protect our oceans and beaches through community engagement, education, and innovative solutions that foster a sustainable future.

Our Vision

A world where our oceans are clean, healthy, and thriving — benefiting all forms of life and ensuring a sustainable future for generations to come.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to protect our oceans and beaches! Whether it's volunteering for local clean-up events, participating in awareness campaigns, or donating to support our initiatives — every small effort counts. Together, we can make a significant impact.
